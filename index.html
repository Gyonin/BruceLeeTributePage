Bruce Lee

Actor, martial artist, poet, husband, father - a great man.

Bruce Lee's mugshot

Here's a short time line of Bruce Lee's life:

  • 1940 - Born on November 27, at the Chinese Hospital in San Francisco
  • 1957 - Begins training Wich Chun under the Wing Chun master Yip Man
  • 1961 - Enroled at the University of Washington, majoring in drama. There he met his future wife Lindy Emery
  • 1964 - Marries Linda Emery with whom he had 2 children, Brandon Lee and Shannon Lee
  • 1964 - Appears in Long Beach International Karate Championship and performs two-finger push-ups and ''One inch punch''
  • 1967 - Founds new martial art - Jeet Kune Do
  • 1972 - Stars in his movie ''Way of the Dragon''
  • 1973 - July 26, dies just few days before the release of his movie ''Enter the Dragon''
